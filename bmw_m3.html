bmw m3

Overview of the BMW M3 The BMW M3 is a high-performance version of the 3 Series, developed by BMW’s M division (Motorsport). It's one of the most iconic sports sedans in the world, known for blending everyday usability with track-ready performance. Key Facts Type: High-performance sports sedan (previously also available as coupe and convertible) Manufacturer: BMW M GmbH First introduced: 1986 Current generation: G80 (launched in 2020) Generations of the BMW M3 E30 M3 (1986–1991) Engine: 2.3L inline-4 (naturally aspirated) Power: ~192–238 hp Highlights: The original M3, built for homologation in racing, legendary among enthusiasts. E36 M3 (1992–1999) Engine: 3.0–3.2L inline-6 Power: ~240–321 hp (depending on market) Notable: More refined and comfortable, yet very capable. E46 M3 (2000–2006) Engine: 3.2L inline-6 (S54) Power: 333 hp Special version: CSL (lighter, sharper, track-focused) Known for: One of the most balanced and loved M3s. E90/E92/E93 M3 (2007–2013) Engine: 4.0L V8 (S65) Power: 414 hp Body styles: Sedan (E90), Coupe (E92), Convertible (E93) Highlights: High-revving V8, unique in the M3 lineage. F80 M3 (2014–2018) Engine: 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 (S55) Power: 425 hp (standard), up to 444 hp (Competition) Major shift: First turbocharged M3, brought massive torque boost. Only available as sedan. G80 M3 (2020–Present) Engine: 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 (S58) Power: 473 hp (standard, manual transmission) 503 hp (Competition, automatic only) Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive or optional xDrive AWD Transmission: 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic Styling: Notable for its large vertical kidney grilles Strengths of the M3 Exceptional driving dynamics Track-ready performance with everyday usability Available with manual transmission (rare in this class) Luxurious interior and advanced tech High resale value and strong enthusiast community Potential Drawbacks Expensive, especially with options Maintenance and repair costs can be high Controversial design (especially the front grille of the G80) Heavier than older M3s due to modern safety and tech features
