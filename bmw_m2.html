BMW M2 (2025)

Overview

The BMW M2 is a high-performance sports coupe that blends power, agility, and premium design. As the smallest member of BMW's M family, the M2 delivers pure driving excitement with rear-wheel drive dynamics and a turbocharged inline-6 engine.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 453 hp
  • Torque: 406 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic
  • 0-60 mph: Approximately 3.9 seconds
  • Top Speed: 155 mph (electronically limited)
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)

Design

The 2025 M2 features a muscular stance, flared wheel arches, quad exhaust tips, and aggressive bumpers. The cabin is driver-focused with M Sport seats, carbon fiber trims, and a digital dashboard.

Technology & Features

  • 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster
  • 14.9-inch central infotainment display (iDrive 8)
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Adaptive M Suspension
  • Harman Kardon sound system (optional)
  • Advanced driver assistance systems
